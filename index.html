About Task Runners

  • Task runners are tools used in web development to automate repetitive tasks like compiling Sass/LESS files, minifying and concatenating JavaScript files, optimizing images, and more.

  • They streamline the development process by performing these tasks automatically, saving developers time and reducing the chance of errors.

Advantages in web development workflows

  • Automation of Repetitive Tasks
  • Consistency
  • Efficiency
  • Customization
  • Dependency Management
  • Optimization
  • Control Integration
  • Community Support

Why Gulp and Grunt?

Both Gulp and Grunt are popular choices for task automation in web development projects.
They offer a wide range of plugins and can be easily integrated into your workflow.

Gulp

  • Code-over-configuration approach
  • Streaming build system
  • Easy to learn and use
  • Active ecosystem

Grunt

  • Configuration-based
  • Task-based
  • Widely adopted
  • Large plugin ecosystem
